Features

Every keystroke,
perfectly human

TypeFlow goes beyond simple auto-type. It replicates the rhythm, mistakes, and flow of genuine human typing.

Natural Timing

Random delays between keystrokes, pauses after punctuation, micro-hesitations before difficult words. Every character lands with human rhythm.

Human-like Typos

Occasional mistakes that get immediately corrected — just like real typing. Adjacent key slips, brief pauses, then a quick backspace fix.

Flexible Control

Start with a countdown timer or global hotkey. Pause mid-flow, adjust speed on the fly, or stop instantly. You're always in command.

Native Experience

Built natively for each platform with OS-specific design language. Lightweight, fast, and feels right at home on your desktop.

How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

1

Paste your text

Copy any text and paste it into TypeFlow. Articles, emails, code — anything you need typed out.

2

Set your speed

Choose your typing speed and style. Fast and efficient, or slow and thoughtful — you control the cadence.

3

Start typing

Switch to any app, hit the hotkey, and watch TypeFlow type your text naturally — character by character.
